ABOUT

Malnutrition's impact is substantial, making children more prone to diseases and deaths. Wasting, stunting, underweight, and overweight conditions are prevalent. This calls for effective interventions and policies.

Dashboard Objective

Revolutionizing Malnutrition Mitigation through Data Analytics

Leveraging the Cognos analytics platform, the dashboard empowers stakeholders with actionable insights. It identifies interventions, allocates resources, and promotes informed decisions. Data-driven visualizations raise awareness, drive policy changes, and enhance nutrition programs' effectiveness, aiming to reduce malnutrition's prevalence and impact worldwide.
